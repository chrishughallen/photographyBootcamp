    About our course

    Photography bootcamp is a 4 week hands-on course designed to bring your photography skills and knowledge to the next level. You will work with veteran photographers who have a combined 50 years experience. By the end of the course, you will have a substantial portfolio, a better understanding of photogrphy, and the know how to become a professional photographer.

    Course

    Over the 4 week period we break each section down to a week. You will go over the 4 sections below, and complete a final assignment for each section.

    • History of Photography

      Learn the basics of film and digital photography. By understanding where it started, you will have much more knowledge and appreciation for how far it has come.

    • Outdoor Photography

      This section focuses on natural landscape photography. You will learn about lighting, tips and techniques for dealing with weather, still objects, and moving subjects.

    • Portrait Photography

      Learn the intimacy involved in portrait photography. We will cover group, individual, and self portrait techniques.

    • Editing

      One of the most important skills a professional photographer can have is editing. We will dive head first into the basics of photoshop and how to improve your work.
